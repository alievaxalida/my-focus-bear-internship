**Who are considered vulnerable populations, and what challenges might they face in digital spaces?**
Vulnerable populations include people with ADHD, Autism, learning difficulties, mental health challenges, or disabilities. In digital spaces, they may struggle with too much information, confusing design, unclear instructions, and stress from notifications or time pressure.

**What ethical considerations are important when working with neurodivergent individuals?**
It is important to be respectful, patient, and inclusive. We should avoid overwhelming design, respect sensory needs, use clear communication, and never judge or blame users for difficulties.

**How can you make interactions and content more accessible for people with ADHD or Autism?**
Use simple language, short sentences, and clear steps. Keep navigation predictable, reduce distractions, and avoid too many choices at one time.

**How can we support neurodivergent team members in a professional setting?**
We can support them by communicating clearly, respecting different working styles, giving clear expectations, and allowing flexibility when possible.
