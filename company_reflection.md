**1. Why Focus Bear was created?**
Focus Bear was created because the founder, Jeremy, has ADHD. He wanted to build a tool to help people stay focused and avoid digital distractions.

**2. What problem is Focus Bear solving?**
It solves the problem of "digital addiction" and lack of routine. Many people get distracted by social media or forget to take healthy breaks. Focus Bear blocks these distractions.

**3. Why is this mission important?**
This mission is important because mental health and focus are essential today. Helping neurodivergent people (like those with ADHD) manage their time improves their quality of life.

**4. How does it align with your values?**
I believe technology should help people, not distract them. I value productivity and discipline, which are the core goals of Focus Bear.

**5. Personal relation to these challenges?**
Yes, I often lose time scrolling through my phone when I should be working. I also find it hard to stick to a morning routine, so I relate to the problems Focus Bear fixes.
