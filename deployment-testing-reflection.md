**What is the difference between a scheduled release, a rollback, and a hotfix?**
Scheduled release: A planned update released at a set time.
Rollback: Going back to the previous version if something goes wrong.
Hotfix: A small, urgent fix released quickly to solve a critical issue.
**What should QA test immediately after a deployment?**
QA should test critical user flows, login, core features, payments (if any), and check that the app starts and works correctly.
**How does QA handle hotfix testing when a critical bug is found after release?**
QA tests only the affected area and related functions. They check that the bug is fixed and no new issues appear.
**What strategies help prevent new bugs from being introduced in urgent fixes?**
Test the fix carefully, do quick regression testing, and communicate closely with developers.
**How do other teams approach post-release monitoring?**
Teams monitor logs, error reports, analytics, and user feedback to find issues quickly.

By testing smartly, communicating quickly, and focusing on high-impact areas, QA helps keep production stable without slowing urgent fixes.
