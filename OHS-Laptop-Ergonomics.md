**What are the risks of using a laptop without an external monitor or keyboard?**
Neck and shoulder strain from looking down
Wrist and hand pain from typing on the laptop keyboard
Back pain from poor posture
Eye strain from screen height or small text

**What ergonomic equipment can improve posture when working on a laptop? (e.g., laptop stands, external keyboards, ergonomic chairs)**
Laptop stand to raise the screen
External keyboard and mouse for better hand position
Ergonomic chair with back support
Footrest if needed

**What adjustments should be made to monitor height, chair position, and desk setup for a healthier workspace?**
Monitor or laptop top at eye level
Chair height so feet are flat on the floor
Desk at elbow height for typing
Sit upright, shoulders relaxed

**What are some daily habits that reduce the impact of prolonged laptop use? (e.g., movement breaks, posture checks, eye strain reduction techniques)**
Take short breaks every 30â€“60 minutes
Stretch arms, neck, and back
Check and correct posture regularly
Use the 20-20-20 rule for eyes (look 20 feet away every 20 minutes for 20 seconds)
