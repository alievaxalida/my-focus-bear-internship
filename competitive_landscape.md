**What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?**
Focus Bear is unique because it combines habit tracking with hardcore blocking. While apps like Freedom only block websites, and Routinery only tracks habits, Focus Bear does both. It doesn't let you access the internet until you finish your morning routine. This "all-in-one" approach is very powerful for self-discipline.

**If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?**
I would choose Focus Bear because it is specifically designed for neurodivergent people (ADHD and Autism). It feels like a personal coach that guides me through my day. It helps reduce "decision fatigue" by telling me exactly what to do next, like taking a break or doing a stretch.

**What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?**
Apps like Tiimo and Routinery have a more colorful and modern User Interface (UI). They use a lot of visual icons and "gamification" (points and levels) to keep users motivated. Focus Bear feels a bit more technical and plain in comparison.

**Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?**
I think Focus Bear could improve its Onboarding UX. The first setup can be a bit complicated for a new user. Adding a "Template Library" where users can easily download ready-made routines (like a "Student Routine" or "Deep Work Routine") would make the app much easier to start using.
