ğŸ” Research & Learn

**What are common cyber security threats in a remote work environment?**
Common threats are phishing emails, fake links, weak passwords, unsecured Wi-Fi, and malware.

**What are best practices for keeping your devices and accounts secure?**
Use strong passwords, update software regularly, lock your computer, and do not share accounts.

**Why is it important to lock your computer when away from your desk?**
Because someone else can access your data and work systems without permission.

**How should you handle phishing attempts and suspicious links?**
Do not click on them, do not download files, and report them to your team or IT.

**What makes a strong password, and why should you use a password manager?**
A strong password is long and uses letters, numbers, and symbols. A password manager keeps passwords safe and helps avoid reuse.

**Why is two-factor authentication (2FA) important, and when should you enable it?**
2FA adds extra security to accounts. You should enable it on email, work tools, and important accounts.
