**What AI tools are typically used for your role?**
AI tools can help with test case writing, bug report suggestions, test data generation, and understanding requirements faster. They save time and help with ideas, but they do not replace testing.
**What are the benefits and risks of using AI in a professional setting?**
Benefits are saving time, working faster, and getting help with analysis. Risks are wrong information, data leaks, and trusting AI too much without checking.
**What types of information should never be entered into AI tools?**
Passwords, personal data, company secrets, client data, financial information, and confidential work documents should never be shared.
**How can you fact-check and validate AI-generated content to ensure accuracy?**
You should review the content yourself, compare it with official documents or requirements, test it in real scenarios, and ask the team if something is unclear.
