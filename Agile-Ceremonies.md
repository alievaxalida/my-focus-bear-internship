# Agile Ceremonies & Team Collaboration

## 🔍 Research & Learn
* **Agile Ceremonies:** Daily Stand-ups for alignment, Sprint Planning for prioritization, Retrospectives for improvement, and Backlog Refinement for task clarity.
* **Asynchronous Collaboration:** Use of tools like Slack, GitHub, and meeting recorders (Fireflies.ai/Read.ai) to bridge time zone gaps between Azerbaijan and Australia.

## 📝 Reflection
* **Communication:** Ceremonies act as a heartbeat for the team, ensuring blockers (like my Test Flight access) are identified and resolved quickly.
* **Role Focus:** As a Manual QA, the **Daily Stand-up** is vital. It’s where I sync with developers about which PRs (Pull Requests) are ready for testing and report any bugs found in the latest build (e.g., version 4.6.1).
