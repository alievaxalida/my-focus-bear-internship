**What are the main Agile ceremonies, and what is their purpose?**
Daily Stand-ups: Short daily meetings to share progress, plans, and blockers.
Sprint Planning (Scrum) / Continuous Prioritization (Kanban): Decide which tasks to do next and plan priorities.
Retrospectives: Meetings to discuss what went well, what can improve, and next steps.
Backlog Refinement: Review and update tasks in the backlog, making them clear and ready for work.

**How do Agile teams collaborate asynchronously and across time zones?**
Teams use tools like Slack, email, or project boards. Updates, comments, and documentation let people work without needing everyone online at the same time.
